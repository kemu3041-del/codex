# Potato Cart Short - Visual Direction

## Style Prompt

9:16 Douyin-style cartoon short with a warm countryside mood. The frame should feel like a hand-cut children's book scene: chunky shapes, visible outlines, bouncy motion, expressive potato character, and simple depth layers. Keep the visuals bright, readable, and playful rather than realistic.

## Colors

- Sky: `#9fdcf6`
- Grass: `#6fbd57`
- Mud road: `#8a5a33`
- Potato skin: `#d99b53`
- Potato shadow: `#9f6133`
- Leaf green: `#2f7d43`
- Caption panel: `#fff4ce`
- Ink: `#3b281d`

## Typography

- Display and captions: system rounded Chinese stack, `"PingFang SC", "Microsoft YaHei", "Arial Rounded MT Bold", sans-serif`
- Use bold, large subtitle text with a soft cream panel and dark brown outline.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use dark cinematic lighting.
- Do not use photorealistic potatoes or muddy road assets.
- Do not use neon, tech gradients, or glass cards.
- Do not make the frame text-heavy; subtitles should be short and readable.
